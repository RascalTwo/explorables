explorables

interactive, mostly AI-generated educational visualizations

This is a collection of explorable explanations — small interactive web pages that try to make an idea click by letting you poke at it, not just read about it.

Most were generated with a custom /viz skill (it lives in this same repo, under skills/viz/), then hand-tuned. Some are serious; plenty are just for fun.

Feel free to explore. Open any page, click around, change things.
