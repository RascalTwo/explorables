3 Your shelf

Your Reading Life, Organized

The library you actually build.

Add a book in seconds — title, author, the page you're on, and total pages. The app turns that into a status (Not Started → In-Progress → Completed) and a progress bar. Filter by status, search your shelf, and hit Update Book as you read.

  • Add books with page-level progress, not just "reading/read"
  • Filter tabs: All · Not Started · In-Progress · Completed
  • Search any title; update your page as you go

4 The capture

Never Lose a Recommendation Again

The habit that makes the app stick.

A friend mentions a book, a podcast drops three, you spot one at 1am. Drop the title and author into the shared Latest & Greatest feed and it's saved for good. See what everyone's recommending — and pull any of it straight onto your shelf with one tap of Add to Library.

  • Capture a rec in two fields: title + author
  • A community feed of what readers are loving right now
  • Add to Library turns a rec into a tracked book instantly

5 Your people

Find Your People with Pods

Where solo reading turns social.

Reading doesn't have to be a solo sport. Pods are small communities built around genres and themes. Spin one up with a name, genre tags, and a summary — or search and request to join someone else's. Your pending requests sit right on the page until you're let in.

  • Create a pod: name, genre tags, and a summary
  • Search and request to join pods that match your taste
  • A "Pending Requests" area tracks where you've knocked
