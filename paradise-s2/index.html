The redraft, in one sentence ALEX isn't a time machine — nobody travels. It identifies a branch where the desired outcome holds and slowly slides pages from other branches into reality until that branch becomes the lived one. The dashed arrows are page-swaps in progress.
Why Xavier bleeds He's User X — ALEX's designated user.
